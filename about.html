About Kevin Li

Kevin is an award-winning magician, celebrated for his modern, meaningful, and memorable performances on America’s Got Talent, Fool Us, and private events worldwide. With roots at the renowned Magic Castle in Hollywood, his contemporary, mind-bending approach to magic and mentalism captivates audiences with each performance. Featured on networks like CBS, Fox, and The CW, Kevin's unique style has garnered millions of online views and a dedicated fanbase. Renowned for creating intimate moments of wonder, he tailors experiences for any audience, from Netflix premieres to Coachella stages. Having performed magic for stars such as Michelle Yeoh, Jamie Foxx, and more, Kevin guarantees unforgettable moments blending modern magic and mentalism.

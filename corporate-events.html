Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kevin's show the right entertainment for my event?

⌄

Kevin’s magic experience offers a new wave of magic whether they’re a veteran of magic shows or seeing one for the first time. Top executives like Sergey Brin (Cofounder of Google) and KS Choi (CEO of Samsung) are just a few who have experienced Kevin’s new style of magic. Kevin’s warm and engaging presence has a way of resonating with even the most skeptical audience members. Kevin will work closely with you to understand your event vision and bring your event to life. No matter the size of your event, Kevin will transform every seat into a front-row experience.

How long is Kevin's program? What is the ideal length of time?

⌄

Kevin can present his experience at your desired length. His full show usually runs 50-60 minutes. Depending on your needs, Kevin will custom tailor the experience for any length of time. Kevin and his team will also provide guidance on creating the perfect timeline for your event. We suggest that you position Kevin’s performance after the meal, so your guests will be comfortably settled and fully focused. We also find it’s best to schedule awards or presentations following the show for maximum effect. Kevin always leaves guests feeling invigorated and upbeat, primed for the actives to come.

What is Kevin's fee?

⌄

Every event will have its own unique features and present its own set of requirements such as travel and audience size. The demand and fees associated with performances may also be affected by timing. The holiday season, for instance, is always in higher demand, which results in a higher fee. Once we understand the details of your event, we will create a custom proposal tailored to your specific needs.

What is the booking process like?

⌄

Once Kevin and his team determine your needs and the details of your event, we will provide you with a custom proposal. Once we’ve signed a contract and locked in a date, my team will be available to assist you with any changes, ideas or requests up until the day of the event. My goal is to provide you with efficient and effortless communication before showtime.
